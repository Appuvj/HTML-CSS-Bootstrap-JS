Sunny Leone, whose real name is Karenjit Kaur Vohra, was born on May 13, 1981, in Sarnia, Ontario, Canada. She comes from a Sikh Indian Punjabi family. Her father was born in Tibet and raised in Delhi, and her mother was from Himachal Pradesh. She had an athletic childhood and attended Catholic school for safety reasons. At 13, her family moved to Michigan, and a year later, to California​ ​. Sunny Leone started her career in the adult entertainment industry, gaining significant fame before transitioning to mainstream cinema. She made her Bollywood debut in 2012 with the film "Jism 2" and has since appeared in several other movies, including "Ragini MMS 2," "Ek Paheli Leela," and "Tera Intezaar." Besides acting, she has participated in Indian reality TV shows like "Bigg Boss" and hosted "MTV Splitsvilla"​ ​. Apart from her film career, Sunny Leone is also known for her activism, supporting causes such as animal rights and cancer research​​. She is married to Daniel Weber, and they have three children: a daughter named Nisha, adopted in 2017, and twin sons, Asher and Noah, born through surrogacy in 2018​ .
